# 🗂️ GitHub Projects Board Setup

Use **GitHub Projects** to visually manage your study tasks like a Kanban board.

---

## Setup Steps

1. Go to your repository on GitHub
2. Click the **"Projects"** tab
3. Click **"New Project"**
4. Choose **"Board"** view
5. Name it: `Surgery 30-Day Tracker`

---

## Recommended Columns

### 📋 To Do
All upcoming topics you haven't started yet.

> Create one card per topic/day. Example: `Day 15 — Appendix & Small Intestine`

### 🔄 In Progress
The topic you are actively studying today.

> Move the card here when you open the day file.

### ✅ Completed
Topics fully studied with confidence ≥ 3.

> Move here after marking the day tracker complete.

### 🔁 Needs Revision
Topics where confidence score was 1 or 2.

> Move here from Completed if you flag it for revision.

### 🏆 Exam Ready
Topics where confidence score is 4 or 5 after revision.

> Final destination for fully mastered topics.

---

## Card Template

When creating a card for each day, include:

```
Title: Day XX — [Topic]
Body:
- Phase: [1/2/3/4]
- Confidence: [after study]
- Link: tracker/dayXX.md
- Notes: [any quick remarks]
```

---

## Labels to Create

| Label | Color | Use For |
|-------|-------|---------|
| `high-yield` | 🔴 Red | Topics that always appear in exams |
| `revision-needed` | 🟡 Yellow | Low confidence topics |
| `phase-1` | 🔵 Blue | Foundations |
| `phase-2` | 🟢 Green | Systems |
| `phase-3` | 🟠 Orange | Consolidation |
| `phase-4` | 🟣 Purple | Revision |

---

## Milestones

Create these milestones to track phase completion:

- `Phase 1 Complete` — due after Day 8
- `Phase 2 Complete` — due after Day 27
- `Phase 3 Complete` — due after Day 27
- `30-Day Challenge Complete` — due after Day 30
